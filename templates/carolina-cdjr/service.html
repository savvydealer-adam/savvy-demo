Service FAQ

For most modern Chrysler, Dodge, Jeep, and Ram vehicles with full synthetic oil, the recommended interval is every 8,000 to 10,000 miles or once a year, whichever comes first. Vehicles used for towing, frequent short trips, or dusty conditions may benefit from more frequent changes. Your vehicle's Oil Change Indicator System will also alert you when it's time. Our technicians will set the reminder during your visit.
While you can have routine maintenance performed at any qualified shop, warranty repairs must be completed at an authorized Chrysler, Dodge, Jeep, or Ram dealership. Bringing your vehicle to Carolina CDJR ensures factory-trained Mopar technicians handle the work, genuine OEM parts are used, and all service records are logged directly in the manufacturer's system -- protecting your warranty and maximizing resale value.
Mopar Vehicle Protection (MVP) offers extended service contracts that cover hundreds of mechanical and electrical components beyond the factory warranty. Plans range from basic powertrain coverage to comprehensive bumper-to-bumper protection. Benefits include roadside assistance, rental car reimbursement, trip interruption coverage, and the ability to have repairs done at any authorized CDJR dealership nationwide. Ask our service advisors about adding MVP to your vehicle.
Routine services like oil changes and tire rotations typically take 45 minutes to an hour. Brake service averages 1 to 2 hours. More involved repairs such as transmission service or diagnostics may take half a day or longer, depending on the issue. We'll always give you a time estimate when you check in, and our service advisors will keep you updated if anything changes. We also offer a comfortable waiting lounge with Wi-Fi and complimentary coffee.
Under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, a manufacturer cannot void your warranty simply for using aftermarket parts. However, if an aftermarket part directly causes a failure, that specific repair may not be covered. To protect your investment, we recommend genuine Mopar parts -- they're engineered specifically for your CDJR vehicle, come with their own warranty, and ensure optimal performance. Our parts department can help you find the right part at a competitive price.
