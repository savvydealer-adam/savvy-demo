About Jarrett Scott Ford

A Dealership Built on Trust

At Jarrett Scott Ford, we believe buying a vehicle should be exciting, not exhausting. Located at 2000 East Baker St. in Plant City, Florida, we've built our reputation on honest pricing, exceptional service, and a team that genuinely cares about putting you in the right vehicle.

Whether you're searching for a rugged new F-150 for the job site, a versatile Explorer for the family, or need expert maintenance from our factory-trained technicians, Jarrett Scott Ford is your one-stop destination. We proudly serve drivers from Plant City, Brandon, Lakeland, Tampa, and throughout Hillsborough County.

Stop by, give us a call at (813) 752-4171, or browse our inventory online. We're here to make your next Ford experience unforgettable.
