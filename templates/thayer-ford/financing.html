Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about financing at Thayer Ford

What credit score do I need to get approved?
We work with lenders who finance all credit levels. While better scores typically get lower rates, we have programs for first-time buyers and those rebuilding credit. Our finance team will work to find the best option for your situation.
Will applying for pre-qualification affect my credit score?
No. Our pre-qualification process uses a soft credit inquiry that does not impact your credit score. A hard inquiry only occurs when you formally submit a credit application after choosing a vehicle.
Can I trade in my current vehicle?
Absolutely! We accept trade-ins on all purchases. You'll receive a fair market value offer for your vehicle, which can be applied directly to reduce your loan amount and monthly payment. You can even owe more than your trade is worth -- we can often roll equity into your new loan.
What documents do I need to bring for financing?
You'll need a valid driver's license, proof of income (recent pay stubs or tax returns), proof of residence (utility bill or lease agreement), and proof of insurance. If trading in, bring your vehicle's title.
Do you offer Ford Credit special APR programs?
Yes! As an authorized Ford dealer, we offer Ford Credit incentive programs including special low APR financing on select new models. These offers change monthly, so contact our finance team for current promotions.
