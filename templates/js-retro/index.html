Our Story

A Tradition of Trust in Plant City

For over two decades, Jarrett Scott Ford has been the dealership Plant City calls home. Founded on the simple belief that buying a vehicle should feel good -- not stressful -- we've built our reputation one handshake at a time.

From the moment you walk through our doors on East Baker Street, you'll feel the difference. No high-pressure tactics. No runaround. Just honest folks who love Ford trucks and cars as much as you do, backed by a service team that treats every vehicle like their own.

Whether you're picking up your first F-150 or bringing in your family's Explorer for its 100,000-mile service, we're here for the long haul. That's not just a tagline -- it's how we've done business since day one.
