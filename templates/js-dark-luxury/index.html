Welcome to Jarrett Scott Ford

For years, Jarrett Scott Ford has been Plant City's hometown Ford dealership, built on a foundation of trust, transparency, and an unwavering commitment to our customers. We're more than just a place to buy a car — we're a part of this community.

Whether you're shopping for a rugged F-150 for the job site, a versatile Explorer for the family, or bringing your vehicle in for expert service, our team is here to make every experience exceptional. We proudly serve drivers from Tampa, Brandon, Bartow, Lakeland, and throughout the greater Tampa Bay area.
