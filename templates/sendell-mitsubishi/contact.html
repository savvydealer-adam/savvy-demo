About Sendell Mitsubishi

Sendell Mitsubishi is a family-owned and operated dealership proudly serving Greensburg and the greater Westmoreland County area. For years, we've been committed to providing an exceptional car-buying experience built on trust, transparency, and genuine customer care.

Whether you're shopping for a new Mitsubishi, a quality pre-owned vehicle, or need expert service and parts, our dedicated team is here to help every step of the way. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers -- not just selling cars.

Visit Sendell Mitsubishi in Greensburg, PA

Sendell Mitsubishi is conveniently located at 5079 US-30 in Greensburg, Pennsylvania, right in the heart of Westmoreland County. We proudly serve drivers from Latrobe, Irwin, Jeannette, Mount Pleasant, Ligonier, and the surrounding communities. Our dealership is easily accessible from Route 30 and just a short drive from the Pennsylvania Turnpike (I-76) and Route 119. Whether you're coming from Pittsburgh, Johnstown, or anywhere in southwestern Pennsylvania, you'll find us easy to reach.

Contact Our Team

Our Sales team is available Monday through Friday from 9AM to 7PM and Saturday from 9AM to 5PM to help you find the perfect new Mitsubishi or pre-owned vehicle. For vehicle maintenance and repairs, our Service and Parts departments are open Monday through Friday from 7:30AM to 6PM and Saturday from 8AM to 4PM. Whether you call, email, or fill out our online contact form, you can expect a prompt response -- typically within the same business day.
