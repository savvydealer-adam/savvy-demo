Our Story

A Tradition of Trust

For decades, Jarrett Scott Ford has served the Plant City community with a simple philosophy: treat every customer the way you would want to be treated. What began as a small-town Ford dealership has grown into one of the Tampa Bay area's most respected automotive destinations, built on a foundation of honesty, transparency, and genuine care for the people who walk through our doors.

Our team of certified Ford technicians, experienced sales advisors, and dedicated finance professionals work together to ensure that every aspect of your experience exceeds expectations. Whether you are shopping for your first vehicle or your fifteenth, we approach every interaction with the same level of attention and respect.

"We do not just sell cars. We build relationships that last for generations."

Located on East Baker Street in the heart of Plant City, our state-of-the-art facility features a comprehensive service center, a fully stocked parts department, and one of the largest new and certified pre-owned Ford inventories in Hillsborough County.

We invest heavily in our people and our facility because we believe the car-buying experience should be as refined as the vehicles themselves. From our comfortable customer lounge to our transparent pricing model, every detail is designed with your satisfaction in mind.

Visit us today and discover why families throughout Plant City, Brandon, Lakeland, and the greater Tampa Bay area choose Jarrett Scott Ford time and again.
