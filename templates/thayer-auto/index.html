The Thayer Story

In 1935, Ralph Thayer opened his first dealership in Bowling Green, Ohio with a simple belief: treat every customer like a neighbor. That single store has grown into one of Northwest Ohio's most respected dealer groups -- six dealerships representing eight of the world's top automotive brands.

Today, with approximately 200 dedicated team members and over 3,000 vehicles sold annually, the Thayer Family Dealerships continues the legacy Ralph started nearly a century ago: integrity, community, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

From the dependability of Chevrolet and Ford to the innovation of Toyota and Honda, we have a vehicle and a team ready to serve you.
