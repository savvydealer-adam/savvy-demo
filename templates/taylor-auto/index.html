The Taylor Story

In 1979, Steve Taylor Sr. opened his first dealership in Toledo, Ohio with a simple belief: treat every customer like family. That single store has grown into Northwest Ohio's largest dealer group -- nine dealerships representing five world-class brands.

Today, with over 350 dedicated team members, the Taylor Automotive Family continues to be guided by the same values that built it: integrity, community, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

From the luxury of Cadillac and Genesis to the reliability of Hyundai, Kia, and Volkswagen, we have a vehicle and a team ready to serve you.

Taylor Cares

The Taylor Automotive Family believes that success is measured not just in vehicles sold, but in the strength of the communities we serve. Through the Taylor Cares initiative, we support local schools, youth sports, charitable organizations, and community events across Northwest Ohio.

From sponsoring Little League teams to supporting food banks and healthcare organizations, we are proud to give back to the communities that have given us so much over the past 45 years.
