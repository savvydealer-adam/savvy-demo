About the Taylor Family

Since 1979, the Taylor name has stood for integrity, community, and automotive excellence across Northwest Ohio.

The Taylor Story

It all started in 1979, when Steve Taylor Sr. opened a single dealership in Toledo, Ohio. His philosophy was simple: treat every customer the way you'd want to be treated, stand behind every vehicle you sell, and invest in the community that supports you.

That single-store operation has grown into Northwest Ohio's largest dealer group -- nine dealerships representing five world-class brands across four cities. Yet the Taylor Automotive Family has never lost sight of the values that built it.

Today, with over 350 dedicated employees, the Taylor family continues to operate with the same hands-on approach and customer-first mentality that Steve Taylor Sr. established more than four decades ago. Every dealership in the Taylor family carries the same commitment to transparency, fair dealing, and exceptional service.

Our Values

The principles that guide every interaction at every Taylor dealership

Integrity First

Transparent pricing, honest assessments, and straightforward communication. We earn trust by being trustworthy, every single time.

Family-First Culture

From our employees to our customers, everyone is part of the Taylor family. We treat people the way we'd want our own family treated.

Community Commitment

We live and work in NW Ohio. Through Taylor Cares, we support schools, charities, youth sports, and organizations that strengthen our communities.

Excellence in Service

Factory-certified technicians, genuine parts, and a relentless focus on getting it right. We service every vehicle as if it were our own.

Continuous Improvement

We invest in our people, our facilities, and our processes. Every year we aim to be better than the last for our customers and our team.

Accountability

We stand behind what we sell and the promises we make. If something isn't right, we make it right -- no excuses, no runaround.

Taylor Cares

We believe that success comes with responsibility. The Taylor Cares initiative formalizes our decades-long commitment to giving back to the communities across Northwest Ohio that have supported us.

From youth sports sponsorships to charitable fundraisers, from supporting local schools to partnering with healthcare organizations, the Taylor family is proud to be an active, invested member of every community we serve.
