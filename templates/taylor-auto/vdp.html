Vehicle Description

The 2025 Kia Telluride SX AWD is the ultimate family SUV, blending premium luxury with rugged capability. Equipped with a powerful 3.8L V6 engine producing 291 horsepower, this Telluride delivers confident performance whether you're navigating Toledo city streets or heading out on a weekend adventure.

This SX trim comes loaded with features including dual panoramic displays, Nappa leather seating with ventilated front seats, a premium Harman Kardon audio system, Highway Driving Assist 2, and Kia's suite of advanced driver-assistance technologies. The Snow White Pearl exterior paired with black leather interior creates a sophisticated and timeless look.
