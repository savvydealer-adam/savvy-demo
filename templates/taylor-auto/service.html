Expert Auto Service Across Northwest Ohio

The Taylor Automotive Family has been a trusted name in Northwest Ohio auto service for over 45 years. With nine service centers spanning Toledo, Perrysburg, Findlay, and Lima, we make it convenient to keep your vehicle running at its best -- no matter which brand you drive.

Five Brands, One Standard of Excellence

Each Taylor service center is staffed with factory-certified technicians who receive ongoing training directly from Cadillac, Genesis, Hyundai, Kia, and Volkswagen. This means your vehicle is serviced by specialists who understand its unique engineering, use the right diagnostic tools, and install genuine OEM parts designed specifically for your car or SUV.

Why Choose a Taylor Service Center?
