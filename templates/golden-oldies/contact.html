Visit Golden Oldies Auto Sales in Hudson, FL

Golden Oldies Auto Sales has served the Hudson, Florida community as a trusted independent dealership since 1991. Conveniently located on SR 52 and US Highway 19, our two locations make it easy for drivers across Pasco County to browse our selection of over 200 quality pre-owned vehicles. Whether you're searching for a reliable daily commuter, a family SUV, or a capable pickup truck, our knowledgeable sales team is ready to help you find the perfect match.

Proudly Serving Hudson, Spring Hill, New Port Richey & Beyond

Our dealership is proud to serve customers from communities throughout the greater Tampa Bay region, including Hudson, Spring Hill, New Port Richey, Port Richey, Trinity, Holiday, Bayonet Point, Tarpon Springs, Land O' Lakes, Wesley Chapel, and Zephyrhills. Both of our Hudson locations offer easy access from US-19, SR 52, and the Suncoast Parkway, making Golden Oldies a convenient stop no matter where you're coming from. Browse our full inventory online before you visit, or stop by today and experience the Golden Guarantee difference in person.

Directions to Our Dealerships

Hudson / NPR Location (10535 SR 52): From US-19, head east on SR 52 for approximately 2 miles. Our lot is on the right just past Little Road. Coming from I-75, take exit 285 onto SR 52 West and continue for about 12 miles.

Spring Hill Location (18913 US Highway 19): Located directly on US-19, just south of the SR 52 intersection in Hudson. Easy access from both Spring Hill and New Port Richey. Look for the Golden Oldies sign on the east side of the highway.
