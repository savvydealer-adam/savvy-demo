Frequently asked questions

Common questions about financing at Thayer Honda

What credit score do I need to get approved?
We work with all credit situations, from excellent to rebuilding. There is no minimum score required to apply. Our finance team partners with multiple lenders to find the best option for your unique situation. The pre-qualification process uses a soft pull that will not affect your credit score.
How long does pre-qualification take?
Most pre-qualification decisions come back within minutes. Once you submit your information, our finance team reviews it and reaches out with your options. The entire process from application to decision is typically completed the same day.
Can I trade in my current vehicle?
Absolutely. We accept trade-ins of all makes and models. Your trade-in value can be applied directly to your down payment, reducing your monthly payment. We offer competitive appraisals and can evaluate your vehicle right at the dealership.
What is the difference between financing and leasing?
Financing means you are purchasing the vehicle and building equity with each payment. Once paid off, you own it outright. Leasing means you are paying to use the vehicle for a set term (typically 24-48 months) with lower monthly payments, then return or purchase it at the end. Our team can help you decide which option works best for your needs and budget.
Do you offer special programs for first-time buyers?
Yes. Honda Financial Services offers programs specifically designed for first-time buyers, recent college graduates, and military personnel. These programs may include reduced rates and special terms. Ask our finance team about eligibility when you apply.
