# Kia Brand Guide

## Official Logo
- **Primary (black)**: `https://www.kia.com/us/content/dam/kia/us/common/kia_logo_black.svg`
- Format: SVG (vector)
- Source: kia.com official CDN

**NEVER fabricate, redraw, or approximate the Kia logo.** Always use the official SVG above.

- **Primary**: Kia Signature (proprietary — not publicly available)

## Brand Tagline
"Movement that Inspires"

## Design Language
- Bold, modern, dynamic
- Dark navy (#05141F) as primary background
- Red (#BB162C) for energy and action
- Clean layouts with movement-inspired angles

## Logo Usage Rules
- Maintain clear space around the logo
- Do NOT alter colors, proportions, or orientation
- On dark backgrounds: use the white version or apply CSS `filter: brightness(0) invert(1)`
- Minimum display height: 24px
