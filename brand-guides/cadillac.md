# Cadillac Brand Guide

## Official Logo
- **Crest (for dark bg)**: `https://www.cadillac.com/content/dam/cadillac/na/us/english/gmit-assets/primary-navigation-icons/cadillac-2dcrest.svg`
  - Apply `filter: brightness(0) invert(1)` for white rendering on dark backgrounds
- Format: SVG (vector)
- Source: cadillac.com official CDN

**NEVER fabricate, redraw, or approximate the Cadillac crest.** Always use the official SVG above.

## Typography
- **Primary**: Cadillac Gothic (proprietary — not publicly available)
- **Web fallback**: DM Sans (Google Fonts) — load weights 300, 400, 500, 600, 700
- Font stack: `'DM Sans', sans-serif`

## Design Language — LUXURY TIER
- Dark, sophisticated palette
- Gold accents used sparingly and elegantly
- Generous spacing (80-100px section padding)
- Uppercase headings with 2-4px letter-spacing
- Thin gold decorative lines as dividers
- This must feel NOTICEABLY more premium than Kia/Hyundai/VW

## Current Models (2025-2026)
Escalade, LYRIQ, CT5, XT4, XT5, XT6, OPTIQ, CELESTIQ

## Vehicle Image CDN
- `https://www.cadillac.com/content/dam/cadillac/na/us/english/models/` and related paths
- Gallery images: `?imwidth=960` for high-res, `?imwidth=300` for thumbnails

## Logo Usage Rules
- The crest is the primary mark — always use the simplified 2021+ version
- On dark backgrounds: white crest (apply invert filter)
- On light backgrounds: dark crest (no filter needed)
- Maintain exclusion zone around the crest
