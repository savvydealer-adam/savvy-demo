# Volkswagen Brand Guide

## Official Logo
- **Primary SVG**: `https://media.vw.com/logos/vw-logo.svg`
- **DealerInspire PNG**: `https://di-shared-assets.dealerinspire.com/public/logos/volkswagen/volkswagen-dark-large-no-space-desktop-logo.png`
- Source: media.vw.com and DealerInspire (authorized dealer platform)

**NEVER fabricate, redraw, or approximate the VW roundel.** Always use the official assets above.

## Colors
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| VW Blue | #001E50 | Primary brand color, headers, CTAs |
| Light Blue | #00B0F0 | Accent, highlights, EV elements |
| White | #FFFFFF | Backgrounds, text on dark |
| Light Gray | #F5F5F5 | Section backgrounds |

## Typography
- **Primary**: VW Head — available from `https://fonts.cdnfonts.com/css/vw-head`
- **Fallback**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Font stack: `'VW Head', 'Inter', sans-serif`
- Load VW Head FIRST — it is publicly available on cdnfonts

## Design Language
- Clean, digital-first, approachable yet premium
- Bright, modern layouts (NOT dark like Cadillac/Genesis)
- VW Blue for authority, white for cleanliness
- Friendly typography, moderate spacing
- EV-forward messaging (ID.4, ID. Buzz prominently featured)

## Current Models (2025-2026)
Atlas, Atlas Cross Sport, Tiguan, Taos, Jetta, ID.4, ID. Buzz, GTI, Golf R

## Logo Usage Rules
- VW roundel is the primary mark (circle with V and W)
- On dark backgrounds: white roundel
- On light backgrounds: VW Blue (#001E50) roundel
- "Volkswagen" wordmark may accompany the roundel
- Maintain clear space equal to the width of the V stroke
