## Typography
- **Display headings**: Cormorant Garamond (Google Fonts) — serif, weights 300, 400, 500
- **Body text**: DM Sans (Google Fonts) — weights 300, 400, 500, 600
- Font stacks:
  - Display: `'Cormorant Garamond', serif`
  - Body: `'DM Sans', sans-serif`

## Design Philosophy — "Athletic Elegance" — ULTRA-LUXURY TIER
- Near-black (#111111) dominant backgrounds
- Copper used only for small accent details (lines, hover states, labels)
- Serif headings (Cormorant Garamond) for editorial luxury feel
- EXTREME letter-spacing on headings (4-8px) and labels (6px)
- Very generous whitespace (100-120px vertical padding)
- Thin 1px accent lines as dividers
- Sharp edges (border-radius: 0) for editorial luxury feel
- This is the MOST PREMIUM template — above Cadillac

## Logo Usage Rules
- The winged emblem is the primary mark
- On dark backgrounds: white version (the SVG should be light on dark)
- Copper accent NEVER on the logo itself — only on UI elements
- "GENESIS" wordmark: ultra-wide letter-spacing (6-10px), thin weight
