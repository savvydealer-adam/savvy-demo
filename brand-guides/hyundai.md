# Hyundai Brand Guide

## Official Logo
- **Primary**: `https://owners.hyundaiusa.com/content/dam/hyundai/us/myhyundai/image/general/logo/hyundai_logo.svg`
- Format: SVG (vector)
- Source: hyundaiusa.com official CDN

**NEVER fabricate, redraw, or approximate the Hyundai logo.** Always use the official SVG above.

## Colors
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Hyundai Blue | #00287A | Primary brand color, headers, CTAs |
| Active Blue | #00AAD2 | Secondary accent, highlights |
| White | #FFFFFF | Text on dark, backgrounds |
| Dark | #1A1A1A | Body text |

## Typography
- **Primary**: Hyundai Sans Text (proprietary)
- **Web fallback**: Inter (Google Fonts) — load weights 400, 500, 600, 700, 800
- Font stack: `'Inter', sans-serif`

## Design Language
- Progressive, innovative, tech-forward
- Clean lines, generous white space
- Blue gradients for hero sections
- Modern, approachable feel

## Current Models (2025-2026)
Tucson, Santa Fe, Palisade, IONIQ 5, IONIQ 6, IONIQ 9, Elantra, Kona, Sonata, Venue, Santa Cruz

- `https://s7d1.scene7.com/is/image/hyundai/` (Scene7 CDN)

## Logo Usage Rules
- Logo must always be displayed in Hyundai Blue (#00287A) or White
- Maintain clear space (half the height of the "H" in HYUNDAI)
- On dark backgrounds: use white version or CSS `filter: brightness(0) invert(1)`
